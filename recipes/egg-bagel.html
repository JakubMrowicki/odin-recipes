Egg Bagel Recipe

Start the day right with this delicious bacon egg and cheese bagel. This New York style bagel is incredibly filling, easy to make, and tastes amazing. Perfect for a satisfying Sunday brunch or breakfast!

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • Egg
  • Bacon
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook bacon
  2. Cook egg
  3. Toast bagel
  4. Butter bagel
  5. Combine
